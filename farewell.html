5th April 2023, Life la marakkavey mudiyatha naal. Yennoda life ye complete ah change panna naal. Nammaloda farewell celebration.
Nammaloda long distance start ana konja naal la ye nammaloda farewell vach changa. Unga amma yenna than poga vendam nu sonnalum nee yennakaga yennakaga matum avanga kuda sanda potu chennai ku vantha.
Literally na la nee varanu therinja thu guthikatha neeram mey illa. Avlo happy ah irunthen. Anniku nee chennai vanthuten nu photos anupunathum na la dress la mathi mathi yethu pota nalla irukkum yethu match agum ippadi la romba romba yosichitey irunthen. Finally ready agi kelambiten.
Aporo vasanth and vijay late panni anga late ah than vanthaney. Na correct ah school la enter agum bothu unga amma exit aguranga. Unna pakka vara excitement la yethumey therila. Avlo excitement la auditorium door open panni unna yen kannu avlo theduchu.
Sudden ah nee yenna thirumbi patha and nanum unna pathen apo iruntha happiness ah measure ye panna mudiyathu. And event start anathula irunthu na unna matummey than pathutu irunthen. Nee sikikurathu nee pesurathu unnoda thodu unnoda dress yella thaiyum pathute irunthen panda.
Aporo nee time aiduchu nu kelambita nu friend pathanga and kelambitiya nu nanum kelambi restroom la ye wait pannitu irunthen nee varuva namma pesalam nu. Ana nee full tensed state la irunthen yenna kannapinna nu thituna.
Unnoda kasta romba perusu nee yennakaga avlo thitu vangi inga vanthu pesa mudiyamma ponnathu unnaku yevlo kasta ma irukkum nu yennaku nallavey purithu ana apo yennala yethuvummey panna mudila panda.
Nee ponathu yennaku oru 15 to 20 mins full blank ah aiduchu panda unnaku na msg pannite nee azhuthathu pathu yen naan la uyiroda irukkuren appdinga level ku mind depressed aiduchu panda.
Yennku munnadi la iruthey nee illa ma irukka mudiyathu nu nalla theriyum. Anniku night nee nambaluku ithula set agathu break up pannikalam innimey namma pesavey vendam nu sollum bothu la yennoda oodambula irunthu uyir ah matum yedukkura mari feel achu panda. Na anniku un kitta avlo beg pannalana unna mari ivlo caring ana pondati and friend kedaichu irukkavey mata panda.
Anniku nanum neeyum hope vidama irunthathu iniki namma life la happy ah irukkurom. Forever I will remember this day.
