Our First Date
Neeyum nanum after 3 years of missing eachother. Yevlo problems vanthalum, oru tharuku oru thar physical ah meet panna mudiyamma, evlo la feel pannunom nu nammaluku than theriyum, yellarum long distance la kastam pa yenna than true ah love pannunalum oru naal athu bore adichidum nu neraiya reels la and some know people la sonanga, Ana oru oru vaati ippadi la solluratha kekum bothu yennku thought la varathu orey oru vishiyam than yennavida unna yaralum love panna mudiyathu and unna vida yenna yaralum love panna mudiyathu. Oru oru vaati sanda podum bothum at night definitely yennakkaga neeyum and unna kaga nanum vitu kuduthu porathu than nammaloda love ivlo strong ah irukkurathu ku main reason. After so many hardships unna pakka vantha journey oda oru kutty story.
Stage 1: Off to Bhubaneshwar
One week munnadila irunthey naan la full ah paranthutey than irunthen yennala words la express panna mudiyatha alavuku happy ah irunthen. Finally 24, December 2025 nanum vasanth thum train la kelambitom. Ana first twist train la yengaloda ticket automatic ah cancel aayiduchu sollitanga, oru nimisham yennakum vasanth kum mini heart attack ye vanthuduchu. Yenna pannurathu yellamey set panni ippo poga mudilanu avlo feel achu panda yetho world ah nikkura mari. Ana yenna yenna problem vanthalum pathukalam nu start pannom journey ah. Vasanth yen kuda iruntha orey oru reason na la than yenna annalum pathukalam nu neraiya problem face panni Bhubaneshwar la 25th December 2025 namma meet pannunom.
